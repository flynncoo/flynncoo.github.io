About me

Since November 2024, I have a postdoc in the Theory group of LIACS in Leiden. From October 2021 - October 2024, I was a PhD student supervised Grégory Ginot at Université Sorbonne Paris Nord, funded by a Marie Sklodowska‑Curie Action grant (No. 945322) held by the FSMP. I spent February-March 2024 as a visitor at Instituto Matemáticas de la Universidad de Sevilla. I completed my master studies in mathematics at Université Paris-Saclay from 2019-2020. My master thesis was on the homotopy theory of the little n-discs operad and supervised by Felix Wierstra and Grégory Ginot. For my undergraduate studies in maths from 2015-2019, I was at Trinity College Dublin with a bachelor thesis on the combinatorics of preLie algebras which was supervised by Vladimir Dotsenko. I'm originally from Cavan, Ireland. A full CV is available on request.

Online professional presence: ArXiv, HAL, Google Scholar, zbMath, MathOverflow, ORCID
