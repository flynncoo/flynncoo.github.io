Oisín Flynn-Connolly

Welcome to my website. I'm a PhD student in the algebraic topology team at Université Sorbonne Paris Nord where my advisor is Grégory Ginot. I'm interested in all things topological but especially in higher commutativity and finding new invariants of algebras and topological spaces. I generally prefer such invariants to be readily calcuable. A lot of my research has involved blending the very classical spectral sequence techniques from the 1960s and 70s with the more modern perspective brought by homotopical algebra and the operadic calculus. I am always happy to chat about maths, feel free to contact me!

I can be contacted at flynncoo[AT]tcd.ie

Keywords: Homotopy, Operads, Massey products, E-infinity algebras
